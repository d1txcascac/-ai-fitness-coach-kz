# Project Vision & Strategic Goals

## Our Mission

The goal of **AI Fitness Coach KZ** is to make professional fitness coaching available to everyone. We think that everyone should be able to exercise effectively no matter what their lifestyle or daily routine is like.

## Breaking the Barriers in Almaty

In our community people face obstacles to staying healthy:

* **Time Constraints**: People who work in offices in Almaty often do not have 2-3 hours to go to the gym because they are very busy.

* **Economic Barriers**: Personal training is very expensive about 7,400 KZT per session many people cannot afford it.

* **Safety Risks**: When people work out at home without a coach they often do the exercises incorrectly which can lead to injuries and long-term problems with their joints.

## Key Strategic Goals

1. **Universal Accessibility**: We want to create a tool that allows people to work out for 5 minutes during their office break with real-time guidance from **AI Fitness Coach KZ**. This way everyone can fit exercise into their lifestyle.

2. **AI Biomechanical Guard**: We will use **YOLOv11-pose** to help people do the exercises correctly like a coach. This will help everyone, from people who do not exercise much to athletes.

3. **Injury Shield**: We are developing a system that can predict when someone is at risk of getting hurt from problems related to sitting at a desk all day like back pain.

4. **Inclusive Design**: We want to make sure that **AI Fitness Coach KZ** is easy to use for everyone in our community so we will translate it into **Kazakh and Russian**.

## Tech Contribution to Kazakhstan

This project is not about fitness it also helps to develop technology in Kazakhstan:

* **Boosting Local AI Ecosystem**: I want to help grow the **Computer Vision** and machine learning expertise in Kazakhstan.

* **Modernizing the Community**: By using models like YOLOv11 I want to show that Almaty can be a center for innovative open-source AI solutions.

* **Talent Development**: We will share our code with developers in Kazakhstan so they can learn from it and build on it.

## Why Open Source?

We are making **AI Fitness Coach KZ** an open-source project because we think that health technology should be available to everyone. By sharing our "Injury Shield" system we hope that the global community will help us make home fitness safer and more advanced, for everyone.
